News Articles

Triangle Blazers and Playin' It Forward Sports, Inc. is pleased to announce the partnership formed with Impact Sports and Fitness in Cary, NC to facilitate this Fall's Annual Fall Basketball School! The Basketball School will begin on August 30th and will continue through the month of October. Blazer coaches will operate the basketball school for kids grades 3 through grades 12 at Impact Sports and Fitness, on Sundays, Tuesdays and Thursdays. Be sure to see the registration form on this page for your child's age group and registration details. Thank you for supporting Playin' It Forward and Triangle Blazers Basketball!
